- Math Operators:

    - * = Times (4 * 3)
    - / = Divide (4 / 2)

    - + = Add
    - - = Subtract

    Order of operations:

    - (numbers in brackets come first)
    - * comes first if no brackets


- Incremental Operators:

       - Score = 10
       - Score += 5
       - Score = 15
       - Score *= 2
       - Score = 30

       - let numLives = 9
       - numLives = -= 8
       - or numLivess-- = 8
       - numLives++ = 9

       i++  vs ++i:

       - let i = 0
       - i++ = 0
       - i = 1

       let i = 5
       result = i++
       result = 5

       let i = 0
       ++i = 1
       i = 1

       let i = 5
       let result = ++i
       result = 6
